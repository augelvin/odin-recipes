Meatball Soup

Description

Indonesian meatball soup for rainy days.

Ingredients

  • 5 meatballs
  • 50 grams of rice noodle or egg noodle
  • 1 stalk of green onion
  • 1 tea spoon of sesame oil
  • Vegetables of choice
  • 3 cups of water

Steps

  1. Chop the green onions into small pieces
  2. Mix all ingredients in a pot
  3. Cook for 20-25 minutes
  4. Serve it on a bowl
  5. Bone apple teeth!
