Steam Egg with Pork

Description

Chinese steam egg with pork. Soft and delicious meal for a cold day.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 100 grams of ground pork
  • 1 stalk of green onion
  • 1 tea spoon of sesame oil
  • 3 table spoon Soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup of water

Steps

  1. Beat up the eggs
  2. Chop the green onions into small pieces
  3. Mix all ingredients in an oven-safe bowl
  4. Steam for 20-25 minutes
  5. Stir fry everything until the rice is hot
  6. Serve it on a plate
  7. Bone apple teeth!
