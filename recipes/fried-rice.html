Fried Rice Recipe

Description

This is an Indonesian style fried rice recipe. Quick and easy recipe when you have leftovers.

Ingredients

  • Cold rice
  • Vegetables oil
  • 2 Eggs
  • Vegetables of choice
  • Protein of choice
  • 5 table spoon oyster sauce
  • 3 table spoon Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Beat up the eggs
  2. Heat up 2 table spoons of vegetables oil
  3. Fry the protein untill golden brown
  4. Mix in the vegetables untill cooked
  5. Make space on th epan to fry the eggs
  6. Fry the eggs like scrambled eggs
  7. Mix in the cold rice
  8. Put in the oyster sauce and soy sauce
  9. Add in salt and pepper to your liking
  10. Stir fry everything until the rice is hot
  11. Serve it on a plate
  12. Bone apple teeth!
